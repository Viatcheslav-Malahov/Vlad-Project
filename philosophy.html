OUR PHILOSOPHY

Face it, to save humanity, you have to solve some really serious problems. And you already know about them: species loss, clean energy, and climate change.

To us, the problem of climate change stands out. Why?

Because if COVID-19 is shocking to you, climate change could be worse.

Since the industrial revolution, economies around the world have been creating private goods that often generate “negative externalities” to common goods, like the environment.

As a result, the amount of carbon dioxide that is locked in the atmosphere is getting so high that some scientists believe humans have already reached a point of no return.

So unless you have a new model of how private goods should be manufactured, consumed and recycled the social cost could become unbearable in the near future.
